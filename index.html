Bikeablebrews.com is a guide for Custis, Fairfax County Parkway, Mount Vernon, and Washington & Old Dominion (W&OD) trail users searching for craft brew refreshment along their walk, run, or ride. Bikeablebrews has identified 84 craft brew establishments, including 19 breweries, brewpubs, and brewery-affiliated venues located close to the trails. All establishments featured here have a substantial craft beer menu and serve beer on the premises. Most have several taps.

Current trail maps include Custis, Mt. Vernon, and the W&OD.

The data provided on this site is for informational and planning purposes only.

Absolutely no accuracy or completeness guarantee is implied or intended. All information on this map is subject to such variations and corrections as might result from a complete title search and/or accurate field survey.

For all map inquiries, contact DT.
For general inquiries, contact thirstyman@bikeablebrews.com
